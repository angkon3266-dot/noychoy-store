# {Product Name}

One-paragraph plain-language summary: what it is, who it's for, why it matters.
This is the first chunk retrieval systems see — make it self-contained.

## Materials & construction
- **Materials:** e.g. brass base, 18k gold plating → link the facts: [gold plating](../materials/gold-plating.md)
- **Stones:** type, cut, setting, count → e.g. [cubic zirconia](../materials/cubic-zirconia.md), pavé set
- **Finish:** e.g. [rhodium plated](../materials/rhodium-plating.md), high polish
- **Dimensions:** length/width/drop in mm; ring sizes available
- **Weight:** grams (per piece / per pair)
- **Closure/fit:** push back, clip-on, adjustable band, chain length…

## Features & benefits
Feature → benefit pairs; the benefit is what copy should lead with.
- Sparkling pavé stones → catches light with every movement
- Rhodium finish → wears daily without tarnishing
- Lightweight build → comfortable for all-day wear

## Emotional story
2–4 sentences of narrative the copywriter can reuse: the moment this piece
belongs to, the feeling it gives, the person wearing it. Write in the brand
voice ([voice-and-tone](../brand/voice-and-tone.md)).

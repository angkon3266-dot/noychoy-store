# Audience & membership economics

## Buyer personas
1. **The self-purchase woman (22–40, urban BD)** — buys for daily wear and
   festive looks; price-checks; trusts COD; browses on mobile (assume mobile
   first in all design/copy).
2. **The gift-giver (partner/family)** — needs confidence more than detail:
   gift occasions, "she'll love it" reassurance, easy WhatsApp questions.
3. **The bride & wedding circle** — bridal sets (~৳6,500), matching pieces,
   urgency around dates; delivery-time certainty matters most.

## Seasonal peaks
Eid (both), wedding season (Nov–Feb), Pohela Boishakh, Valentine's Day,
anniversaries year-round.

## Membership program (real numbers — safe to quote)
- Earning: **1,000৳ spent = 100 points** (0.1 pt per ৳, on delivered orders).
- Redeeming: **100 points = ৳5** off (redeem in steps of 100).
- Reviews: **200 points** per approved review, **+100** with a photo.
- Tiers by lifetime points: **Silver** (0+, 1× earning) → **Gold** (3,000+,
  1.25× + early access to drops) → **Platinum** (10,000+, 1.5× + priority
  support & gifts).
- Members also get member pricing on select pieces, personalized offers, and
  web-push/bell notifications for drops and price changes.

## Segments the platform tracks (usable for targeting)
RFM auto-segments (champions, loyal, at-risk, etc.), win-back automation for
lapsed buyers (default: no order in 60 days → automatic offer), and custom
admin segments. Campaign copy can and should differ per segment — see
[marketing/channels-and-crm](../marketing/channels-and-crm.md).

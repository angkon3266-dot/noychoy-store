# Brand identity — Meridian Éclat

## Who we are
Meridian Éclat (meridianeclat.shop) is a Bangladeshi online fine-jewelry
store: handpicked earrings, rings, necklaces, bracelets, bangles and anklets,
delivered nationwide with **cash on delivery** — pay only when the piece is in
your hands. Positioning: **accessible everyday luxury** — pieces that look and
feel premium (cubic zirconia brilliance, rhodium and gold finishes, pearls) at
prices an ordinary buyer can say yes to (most pieces ৳1,000–2,000; bridal sets
around ৳6,500).

## The promise (use these, they're true)
- **Cash on delivery, nationwide** — no advance payment, no risk.
- **Handpicked & quality-checked** pieces.
- **Delivery via Steadfast courier**, inside and outside Dhaka.
- **Membership is free and pays**: member prices, loyalty points on every
  order, points for reviews, tier perks (see [audience](audience.md)).

## Tagline family
"Jewelry that tells your story" · "Shine with timeless elegance" ·
"Crafted to be treasured". New taglines should stay in this emotional register:
personal story + lasting shine, never tech-y or discount-led.

## What we are not
- Not a luxury house — we never fake heritage or use "solid gold/diamond"
  claims for plated/CZ pieces (see material files for allowed claims).
- Not a bazaar — no ALL-CAPS urgency spam, no fake countdowns, no "80% OFF!!"
  screaming. Discounts are framed as membership/occasion privileges.

## Operating facts AI must know
- Currency: **BDT (৳)**. Payment: COD (online payments planned).
- Store name/branding is configurable in the admin panel — in generated copy
  always use the store name, never hardcode a different brand.
- Contact: WhatsApp button on every product page; member notifications via
  web push, bell, SMS and email.

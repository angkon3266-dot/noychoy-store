# Gold plating

## What it is
A layer of real gold electroplated onto a base metal (usually brass or
alloy). Delivers the warm gold look and glow at accessible prices. Thickness
varies by piece; it is **not** solid gold.

## Why we use it
The bridal and festive market in BD wants warm gold tones; plating brings
that look to ৳1,000–7,000 price points instead of lakh-taka solid gold.

## Care instructions
Gold plating is softer than rhodium: last on, first off (after makeup and
perfume); wipe gently after wear — don't scrub; store airtight/dry; remove
before bathing, swimming, workouts. Treated well, the color stays rich
through seasons of occasional festive wear.

## Copywriting notes
- Sell: "gold plated", "rich gold glow", "bridal gold tone".
- **Never**: "solid gold", "22k gold" (unless the piece truly is), "asli
  shona". If a karat tone is used ("18k gold plated"), the word *plated*
  must stay attached.

## FAQ
**Is it real gold?** The plating layer is real gold over a quality base
metal — that's how the price stays accessible.
**How long does the color last?** With the care steps above, seasons of
regular wear; heavy daily abrasion shortens any plating's life.

# Pearls

## What it is
Our pearl pieces use quality simulated/shell pearls: uniform, high-lustre
spheres with the classic soft glow, far more consistent (and affordable)
than natural sea pearls. [CONFIRM per product: shell pearl vs glass-based —
note it in the product file's Materials section]

## Why we use it
Pearls read as timeless and bridal in BD fashion; simulated pearls give the
look with uniform size/color and no fragility premium.

## Care instructions
Pearls (real or simulated) hate chemicals: perfume and hairspray before
wearing, wipe with a soft cloth after; store separately — metal edges
scratch pearl coating first.

## Copywriting notes
Sell: "lustrous pearls", "pearl glow", "timeless". Avoid "natural/sea
pearl" claims. "Pearl" alone is acceptable trade usage for simulated pearls
in fashion jewelry, but if a customer asks directly, answer honestly.

## FAQ
**Are these real pearls?** They're high-quality simulated pearls — uniform
lustre and shape, made to be worn often at a price that makes sense.

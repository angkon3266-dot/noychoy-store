# Payments

- **Current method: cash on delivery (COD) only** — the customer pays the
  Steadfast delivery person on receipt. No advance, no card needed. This is
  the store's strongest trust lever; lead with it in copy.
- Coupons, member prices, loyalty-point redemption (100 pts = ৳5) and
  personalized offers all apply at checkout and reduce the COD amount due.
- Order totals can be amended by the admin after placement (e.g. agreed
  discount or added gift wrap) — the customer pays the amended total.
- **Planned:** online payments (bKash / Nagad / SSLCommerz) including
  partial advance on COD to reduce fake orders. Not live — never tell a
  customer they can pay online today.

# Customer FAQ

Canonical answers. Support replies and PDP FAQ blocks draw from here — keep
one truth per question.

## Ordering & payment
**Do I pay in advance?** No — cash on delivery nationwide. You pay the
delivery person when the parcel arrives.
**How do I order?** Add to cart → checkout with name, phone, address. No
account needed (but members earn points — it's free).
**Can I order on WhatsApp?** Yes — every product page has a WhatsApp button
with the piece pre-filled.

## Product & quality
**Is it real gold / real diamond?** Our pieces use quality plating and
cubic zirconia stones — premium look without the premium price. We say so
honestly (see the material pages for exact wording).
**Will it tarnish?** Rhodium/gold plated pieces resist tarnish with basic
care — see care instructions on each product page.
**Do colors match the photos?** We shoot true to color; minor variation from
screen settings is possible.
